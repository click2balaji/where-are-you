 ⏰

 📋
Swiss Re Institute — Insurance ReportsGlobal data on insurance coverage gaps and financial protection by country.
🤖 Built With AI — What That Means
Heads up: The questions, score weightings, and country benchmarks in this quiz were curated with the help of AI — specifically Claude by Anthropic. AI is good at synthesising large amounts of data, but it can make mistakes, miss nuance, or reflect biases in the underlying data.

This is not a certified scientific assessment. No single quiz can fully capture the complexity of a human life. Treat your result as a thoughtful estimate — a starting point for reflection, not a final verdict.
💙 Why We Built This
We built this to make you think — not to make you feel bad, and definitely not to judge anyone. Life is complicated and wildly unfair across the globe.

Whatever your result, the only direction worth moving is forward. Even one small change — an emergency fund, a health plan, a new skill — can shift where you stand.

No one is responsible for the output of this quiz except the data it draws from and the choices you make next.
